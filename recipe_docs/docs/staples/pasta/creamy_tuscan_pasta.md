# Creamy Tuscan Pasta

> *A quick note about this dish. Why is it good? What does it pair perfectly with?*

**Prep time:** `15 mins` | **Cook time:** `20 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---

## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

- Spaghetti | 3/4 packet
- Chicken Thighs | 450g
- Butter | 1 tbsp
- Garlic | 3 cloves
- Tomato Puree | 1 tbsp
- Chicken Stock | 1/3 cup
- Heavy Cream | 2/3 cup
- Sun Dried Tomatoes | 90g
- Parmesan | 90g
- Baby Spinach | 2 tbsp
- Fresh Basil | 2 handfuls

**Sauce / Spices**

- Smoked Paprika 
- Oregano
- Onion Powder
- Salt
- Black Pepper

---

## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep:** Mince the garlic, finely chop basil, grate the parmesan
2. **Cook the chicken:** Marinate the thighs with the specified spices and brown it on both sides in olive oil - let it rest after cooking before cutting it 
3. Make the pasta: Bring a pot of salty water to a boil, toss in the pasta and cook for 1 less minute than the package instructions
4. **Make the sauce:** 
	- In the same pan melt the butter, saute the garlic
	- fry tomato puree
	- add the stock and cream 
	- add the sundried tomatoes, basil and parmesan
	- stir in the spinach
	- toss in pasta and chicken
5. **Serve:** Serve hot with garlic bread if youre feeling fancy and garnish with more cheese and any leftover basil

---

## 💡 Notes & Variations

* **Storage:** 2 days in the fridge?
* **Substitutions:** Don't have basil or olive? Its not that deep, skip the basil and use any oil you have
* **For the stock:** I use stock cubes and dissolve it in some boiling water
