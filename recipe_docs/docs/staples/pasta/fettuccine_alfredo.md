# Fettuccine Alfredo

> *Fettuccine Alfredo Rich, creamy, and indulgent. A simple yet luxurious Italian-American classic.*

**Prep time:** `5 mins` | **Cook time:** `15 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---

## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

- Fettuccine pasta | 300g
- Heavy cream | 1 Cup
- Unsalted butter | 1/3 Cup
- Parmesan cheese | 1 Cup (freshly grated)
- Garlic | 2 Cloves (minced)
- Chicken thighs | 450g

**Sauce / Spices**

- Salt
- Pepper
- Thyme

---

## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Boil**: Cook fettuccine in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain, saving a little pasta water.
2. **Protein**: Salt and pepper the chicken and cook in oil till golden brown. Let it rest before cutting it into bit sized pieces
3. **Melt**: In a large skillet over medium-low heat, melt the butter. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
4. **Cream**: Whisk in the heavy cream and let it simmer gently for 2-3 minutes until slightly thickened.
5. **Cheese:** Reduce heat to low. Gradually whisk in the parmesan cheese until completely smooth. Add the chicken and toss in the pasta to coat.

---

## 💡 Notes & Variations

- [**Mushroom Alfredo**:](mushroom_alfredo.md) Add mushrooms and cook until golden brown before adding the garlic
- **Consistency**: If the sauce is too thick, use 1-2 tablespoons of the reserved pasta water to thin it out.
- **Flavor Boost:** Add a pinch of thyme for extra depth.
