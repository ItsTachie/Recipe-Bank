# Mac & Cheese

> *Mac and cheese is the ultimate culinary hug combining American, Italian and French culinary knowledge into a single beloved comfort food.*

**Prep time:** `5 mins` | **Cook time:** `15 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---
## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

-  Elbow macaroni 
-  Cheddar cheese | 2 cups
- Milk | 2 cups
- Flour | 2tbsp
- Butter | 2tbsp
- Chicken thighs | 450g

**Sauce / Spices**

-  Garlic powder
- Paprika
- Salt
- Black Pepper

---
## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Boil:** Cook the macaroni in salted boiling water until _al dente_. Drain and set aside. (keep some pasta water)
2. **The Roux:** In the same pot, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1 minute until it smells slightly nutty but hasn't browned.  
3. **The Base:** Gradually whisk in the milk. Simmer for 3–5 minutes, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon.
4. **The Melt:** Turn off the heat. Stir in the garlic powder, paprika, and 1.5 cups of the cheese until completely smooth and velvety.
5. **The Finish:** Fold the cooked pasta back into the sauce. If it's too thick, add a splash of milk/pasta water. Top with the remaining cheese and serve immediately.
6. Pan-fry some chicken thighs and toss in a buffalo sauce (butter + hot sauce + garlic powder)

---
## 💡 Notes & Variations

* **Variations:** Add other types of cheese; parmesan, gouda, whatever tickles your fancy.
