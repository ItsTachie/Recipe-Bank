# Spaghetti Bolognese

> *A deeply savory, slow-simmered Italian meat sauce served over long pasta*

**Prep time:** `15 mins` | **Cook time:** `45 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---

## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

- Spaghetti | 300g
- Ground beef | 400g
- Crushed tomatoes | 1 Can (400g)
- Onion, Carrot, Celery | 1/2 cup each (finely diced)
- Garlic | 3 Cloves (minced)
- Tomato paste | 1 Tablespoon
- Beef stock | 1 cube

**Sauce / Spices**

- Salt
- Pepper

---

## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Soffritto**: Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Sauté the diced onion, carrot, and celery for 5-7 minutes until soft. Add garlic.
2. **Brown**: Add the ground beef, breaking it apart with a spoon. Cook until fully browned.
3. **Simmer**: Stir in tomato paste, then add crushed tomatoes and beef stock .Lower heat, cover, and gently simmer for at least 30 minutes.
4. **Serve**: Cook spaghetti until al dente. Toss the pasta directly into the rich meat sauce before serving.

---

## 💡 Notes & Variations

- **Simmer Time**: Bolognese gets better the longer it cooks. If you have time, let it simmer on low for up to 2 hours.
- **Feeling fancy**: Reduce red wine before adding the tomatoes and stock then simmer
- **Variation**: A splash of milk balances the acidity of the tomatoes and helps create a rich, silky sauce.
