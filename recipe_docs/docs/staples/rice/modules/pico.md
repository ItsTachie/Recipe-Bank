# Pico de Gallo

> *A bright, acidic "salsa" that provides the perfect contrast to heavy, savory chicken and rice.*

**Prep time:** `10 mins` | **Cook time:** `0 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---
## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

- Tomatoes (seeded and diced) | 3 medium
- Red/white onion (finely diced) | 1 small
- Garlic | 2 cloves (minced)
- Fresh cilantro (chopped) | 1/2 cup
- Jalapeno | 1 large

**Sauce / Spices**

- Lime (juiced) | 1
- Salt | 1/2 tsp
- Pepper

---
## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep**: Dice everything into small, uniform cubes.
2. **Combine**: Mix all ingredients in a glass bowl.
3. **Serve**: Let it sit for 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld before serving.
