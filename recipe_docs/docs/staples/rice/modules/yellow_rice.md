# Yellow rice 

> *A vibrant, earthy, and aromatic rice that looks as good as it tastes.*

**Prep time:** `5 mins` | **Cook time:** `20 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---
## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

-  Long grain white rice | 1 cup
- Stock | 2 cups
- Onions | medium (finely diced)
- Garlic | 3 cloves
- Butter 

**Sauce / Spices**

- Turmeric 
- Bay leaves
- Cumin
- Salt
- Pepper 

---
## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep**: Rinse the rice until water runs clear. Dice the onion and mince the garlic.
2. **Cook**: Sauté onions and garlic in the butter until translucent, then add the spices. Fry for 2 minutes until everything is well incorporated.
3. **Combine**: Stir in the rice to coat every grain in the mixture. Pour in the stock, bring to a boil, then cover and simmer on low for 15 minutes.
4. **Serve**: Let it rest with the lid on for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork and serve.

---
## 💡 Notes & Variations

 **Substitutions:** Use water and a bouillon cube if you don't have liquid stock
