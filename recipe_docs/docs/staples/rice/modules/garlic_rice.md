# Garlic Butter Rice

> *It’s simple, aromatic, and goes with absolutely everything.*

**Prep time:** `5 mins` | **Cook time:** `20 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---
## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

-  Long grain white rice | 1 cup
- Stock | 2 cups
- Butter 

**Sauce / Spices**

- Garlic | 4 cloves
- Fresh parsley | 1tbsp (chopped)

---
## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep**: Mince the garlic very finely.
2. **Cook**: Melt 2 tbsp of butter in the pot. Sauté the garlic on low heat for 1-2 minutes—do not let it brown, or it will turn bitter.
3. **Combine**: Stir in the rice, then add the stock. Bring to a boil, cover, and simmer on low for 15 minutes.
4. **Serve**: Stir in the final 1 tbsp of butter and the fresh parsley right before serving.

---
## 💡 Notes & Variations

 **Substitutions:** Use water and a bouillon cube if you don't have liquid stock
