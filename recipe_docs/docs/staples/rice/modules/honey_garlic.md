# Honey Garlic Chicken

> *Sticky, sweet, and intensely savory. This glaze creates a restaurant-quality finish that clings perfectly to the chicken.*

**Prep time:** `10 mins` | **Cook time:** `12 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---
## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

- Chicken thighs (cubed) | 500g
- Oil 

**Sauce / Spices**

- Honey | 3 tbsp
- Soy sauce | 2 tbsp
- Garlic (minced) | 3 cloves
- Ginger (minced) | 1 tsp
- Cornstarch

---
## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep**: Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and mince the garlic.
2. **Cook**: Sear chicken (seasoned) in a hot pan with oil until golden and cooked through (8-10 mins).
3. **Combine**: Lower the heat. Add the garlic and ginger. Pour in the honey, soy sauce. Stir constantly for 2-3 minutes until the sauce reduces into a thick, sticky glaze.
4. **Thicken**: Pour in a cornstarch slurry to thicken the sauce if too watery
5. **Serve**: Serve immediately while the glaze is glossy and hot.
