# Red rice 

> *The tomato paste caramelizes to create a deep, umami-rich base that makes the rice feel like a main event rather than just a side?*

**Prep time:** `5 mins` | **Cook time:** `20 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---
## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

-  Long grain white rice | 1 cup
- Stock | 2 cups
- Onions | medium (finely diced)
- Garlic | 3 cloves

**Sauce / Spices**

- Tomato paste | 2 tbsp
- Paprika | 1 tsp
- Salt-Pepper 
- Bay leaves
- whatever spices you like tbh

---
## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep**: Rinse the rice until water runs clear. Dice the onion and mince the garlic.
2. **Cook**: Sauté onions in a pot with oil until translucent, then add tomato paste and spices. Fry for 2 minutes until the paste turns a deep, dark red.
3. **Combine**: Stir in the rice to coat every grain in the red oil. Pour in the stock, bring to a boil, then cover and simmer on low for 15 minutes.
4. **Serve**: Let it rest with the lid on for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork and serve.

---
## 💡 Notes & Variations

 **Substitutions:** Use water and a bouillon cube if you don't have liquid stock
