# Creamy Coleslaw

> *Cool, crunchy, and refreshing. The perfect "fire extinguisher" for spicy buffalo or salty BBQ chicken.*

**Prep time:** `10 mins` | **Cook time:** `0 mins` | **Servings:** `2-4`

---
## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

- Red and Green Cabbage (shredded) | 2 cups
- Carrot (grated) | 1

**Sauce / Spices**

- Mayonnaise | 1/2 cup
- Apple cider vinegar | 1 tbsp
- Sugar | 1 tsp
- Black pepper
- Salt

---
## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep**: Shred the cabbage and grate the carrot.
2. **Combine**: Whisk the mayo, vinegar, and sugar in a large bowl. Fold in the vegetables.
3. **Serve**: Best served chilled.
