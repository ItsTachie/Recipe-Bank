# BBQ Glazed Chicken

> *Smoky, bold, and slightly charred. This is the perfect way to get "grilled" flavors using just a standard kitchen pan.*

**Prep time:** `5 mins` | **Cook time:** `15 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---
## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

- Chicken thighs (skin-on preferred) | 500g

**Sauce / Spices**

- Ketchup | 1/2 cup
- Smoked paprika | 1 tsp
- Brown sugar | 2 tbsp
- Honey
- Soy sauce
- Worcestershire sauce
- salt and pepper 

---
## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep**: Pat chicken dry. Mix the BBQ sauce, paprika, and sugar in a small bowl.
2. **Cook**: Fry chicken skin-side down until crispy. Flip and cook until done.
3. **Combine**: Combine the ingredients for the sauce. Simmer it to reduce and mix in the chicken.
4. **Serve**: Rest for 2 minutes then slice and serve.
