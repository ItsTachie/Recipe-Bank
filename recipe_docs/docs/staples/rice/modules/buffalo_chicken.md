# Buffalo Chicken

> *A tangy, spicy classic. The butter mellows the heat of the vinegar-based hot sauce to create a rich, addictive coating.*

**Prep time:** `5 mins` | **Cook time:** `12 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---
## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

- Chicken thighs or breast (strips) | 1 lb
- Oil | 1 tbsp

**Sauce / Spices**

- Frank's RedHot (or similar) | 1/2 cup
- Cold butter (cubed) | 1/4 cup
- Garlic (minced) | 1/2 tsp
- White Vinegar
- Worcestershire sauce
- Sugar/honey

---
## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep**: Slice the chicken into long strips.
2. **Cook**: Pan-fry the chicken until crispy and golden.
3. **Combine**: In a separate small pan (or the same pan after draining fat), whisk the hot sauce and garlic powder and a splash of vinegar. Add in some sugar/honey, Worchester sauce . Bring to a simmer, then whisk in cold butter one cube at a time until thick.
4. **Serve**: Toss the cooked chicken in the sauce until fully coated.
