# Classic Potato Salad

> *Hearty and filling. This turns your chicken and rice into a massive feast that feels like a weekend celebration.*

**Prep time:** `10 mins` | **Cook time:** `15 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---
## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

- Potatoes (peeled and cubed) | 2 large
- Red onion | diced
- Cucumber | diced

**Sauce / Spices**

- Mayonnaise | 1/2 cup
- Yellow mustard | 1 tsp
- Paprika | 1/2 tsp
- Dill
- Vinegar
- Salt

---
## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep**: Boil potato cubes in salted water until tender (about 10-12 mins). Drain and cool.
2. **Combine**: Mix mayo, mustard, vinegar, dill, and paprika, salt. Gently fold in the cooled potatoes.
3. **Serve**: Top with an extra sprinkle of paprika for color.

---
## 💡 Notes & Variations

- **Variations:** Make a mixture of Greek yogurt and mayo for a lighter version.
