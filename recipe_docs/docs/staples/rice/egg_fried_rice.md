# Egg fried rice 

> *One of the fastest, most satisfying meals you can make.*

**Prep time:** `5 mins` | **Cook time:** `15 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---
## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

-  Eggs | 4
-  Rice | 1 cup (day old)
- Onion | 1 medium 
- Garlic | 4 cloves
- Bacon | 2-3 strips

**Sauce / Spices**

-  Soy Sauce 
-  Sesame oil 
- White pepper

---
## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep:**  Chop the onions, mince the garlic.
2. **Protein and Eggs:**  Cook the bacon until golden brown then cut into little bits and set aside. Whisk the eggs in a bowl with some salt then scramble them  in the same pan then set aside
3. **Aromatics:** Cook the onions until their translucent then add the garlic for a further 30 seconds
4. **Combine:** Once aromatics are fragrant add in the rice, break it up and fry it for a bit then add the eggs and bacon back in. 
5. **Finish**: Add in soy sauce, sesame oil and white pepper. Give it a final high-heat stir, and serve.

---
## 💡 Notes & Variations

* **Substitutions:** Don't have bacon? Use any protein of your liking or don't bother with it at all.
* **Make it Spicy:** Add chili flakes during step 4 to turn up the heat.
* **Umami boost:** Add a teaspoon of oyster sauce or MSG for restaurant-style depth
