### 💡 General Tips & Ratios

* **The Rice Ratio:** 1 cup rice to 2 cups liquid (Stock is always better than water!).
* **The Rest:** Let the chicken rest for 5 mins before slicing to keep it juicy.
* **The Mix:** Sautéing your rice in the "chicken fat" (if pan-frying) probably makes it taste better.
