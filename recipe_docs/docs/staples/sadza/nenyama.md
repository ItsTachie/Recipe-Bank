# Sadza neNyama

> *A hearty Zimbabwean classic — tender meat in a rich tomato broth served with sadza and greens for the ultimate home-cooked meal.*

**Prep time:** `10 mins` | **Cook time:** `40 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---

## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

- Kale/Collard greens | 900g
- Onion | 1 medium onion
- Garlic | 4 cloves
- Tomato paste | 2 tsp
- Fresh/canned chopped tomatoes | x2
- Stock | 1 cube
- Chicken/Beef | 450g
- Bisto/Cornstarch

**Sauce / Spices**

- Salt
- Pepper
- Paprika
- Onion powder
- Garlic powder

---

## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep:** Chop the onions, mince the garlic, chop the greens
2. **Make the soup:** Season and brown the meat and set aside.
3. **Aromatics**: Fry the onions, garlic and tomato paste then add the tomatoes and stock and let that simmer for a bit
4. **Combine:** Mix in a cornstarch slurry to the soup and add the meat back in and let that simmer to get the meat tender
5. **Make the sadza** While the soup simmers make [sadza](sadza.md)
6. **Fry the veg**: Fry the veggies and salt them while they're still hot
7. **Serve:** Serve the sadza and relish hot and enjoy

---

## 💡 Notes & Variations

- **If using beef:** Boil the meat with some spices to tenderize it before frying
