# Sadza nemaveggie nenyama

> *A quick note about this dish. Why is it good? What does it pair perfectly with?*

**Prep time:** `10 mins` | **Cook time:** `40 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---
## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

- Kale/Collard greens | 900g
- Onion | 1 medium onion
- Garlic | 4 cloves
- Tomato paste | 2 tsp
- Fresh/canned chopped tomatoes | x2
- Stock | 1 cube
- Chicken/Beef | 450g
- Bisto/Cornstarch 

**Sauce / Spices**

- Salt 
- Pepper
- Paprika
- Onion power
- Garlic powder

---
## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep:** Chop the onions, mince the garlic, chop kale
2. **Cook:** Bring some oil to medium high
	- Make [sadza](sadza.md)
	- Fry the kale in some oil and salt it while hot 
	- Cook and brown the meat 
	- In the same pan cook the onions, the moisture should release the fond and once they are translucent add the garlic 
	- Once garlic is fragrant add some tomato puree and season the mixture and fry
	- Add the chopped tomatoes and the stock and let that simmer and cook for 10 minutes 
	- Add a cornstarch slurry and the meat back in and let that simmer to get the meat tender 
3. **Combine:** You can combine the kale into the soup if you want or serve them separately
4. **Serve:** Serve the sadza and relish hot 

---
## 💡 Notes & Variations

* **If using beef:** Boil the meat with some spices to tenderize it before frying
