# Goulash

> *A hearty soup. That goes well with rice. Kinda similar to a stew you'd make for [Sadza nenyama](staples/sadza/nenyama.md) just with potatoes and carrots added to it. A dish found all around the globe going by different names, in Hungary its goulash (Gulyás) in Zimbabwe its simply 'beef stew'*

**Prep time:** `10 mins` | **Cook time:** `60 mins` | **Servings:** `3`

---

## 🛒 Ingredients

**Main**

- Onion | 1 medium onion
- Garlic | 4 cloves
- Tomato paste | 2 tsp
- Potatoes
- Carrots
- Tomatoes (chopped) | 1 can
- Stock | 2 cubes
- Beef | 500g

**Sauce / Spices**

- Salt
- Pepper
- Paprika
- Bay Leaves

---

## 🍳 Instructions

1. **Prep:** Chop the onions, mince the garlic, cube the potatoes and carrots
2. **Brown the meat:** Season and brown the meat. Add and fry the onions, garlic and tomato paste. Season with the spices above and add a bay leaf
3. **Make the soup:** Add the tomatoes let that incorporate then add stock and let that simmer for 40 minutes
4. **Add vegetables:** Add in the potatoes and carrots and simmer until their fork tender
5. **Garnish:** Just before finishing toss in some chopped parsley if you're feeling fancy

---

## 💡 Notes & Variations

- **Making it spicier:** I like to add some chili peppers to the seasoning to give it a kick.
- **Variations:** Green and red peppers are also welcome to be added in step 3.
